About me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), starting from September, 2022. I received my MEng degree in 2022 from the School of Software and BNRist, Tsinghua University , under supervision of Prof. Xuan Ding and Prof. Zheng Yang. I was also very fortunate to work with Prof. Chunhui Duan (Postdoc@Tsinghua, now Associate Professor@BIT). Before that, I received my BEng degree in 2019 from Zhejiang University of Technology, under supervision of Prof. Kaikai Chi.

Research

I am pursuing the combination of theoretical modeling and system implementation to push forward the development of IoT applications. My research interests include

  • IoT Secuirty: IoT Device Authentication (e.g., RFID tag Authentication), RF-based Person/Object Identification, etc

  • Mobile and Ubiquitous Computing: RF-based Sensing for Smart Applications

  • Energy Harvesting for IoT systems: Wireless Charging, Rechargeable Sensor Networks, etc
