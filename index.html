About me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under supervision of Prof. Qian Zhang, starting from September, 2022. I received my MEng degree in 2022 from the School of Software and BNRist, Tsinghua University, under supervision of Prof. Xuan Ding and Prof. Zheng Yang. I was also very fortunate to work with Prof. Chunhui Duan (Postdoc@Tsinghua, now Associate Professor@BIT). Before that, I received my BEng degree in 2019 from Zhejiang University of Technology, under supervision of Prof. Kaikai Chi.

Research

I am pursuing the combination of theoretical modeling and system implementation to push forward the development of IoT applications. My research interests include

  • Multimodal LLMs: LLM-based Education, MLLMs/VLMs for Real-world Industrial Systems, Embodied AI with RLHF, etc.

  • Mobile and Ubiquitous Computing: Multi-modal Sensing, Low-cost Spectral Imaging, Domain Generalization for Mobile Applications, etc.

  • RFID and Wireless Sensing: RFID Authentication, Robust RF-based Sensing, etc.

  • Energy Harvesting for WSNs & IoT systems: Wireless Charging, Rechargeable Sensor Networks, etc.
